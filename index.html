About Me

Hi! I am a freelance Front End Developer with more than 2 years of experience. I am passionate about building interactive and responsive web sites. I constantly improve my skills, do not like to stop there. Every day I am learning the latest technologies in web development.
I started, as a lot of others did, re-coding web sites that I liked to see how experienced developers did it. Later, when I acquired a bit of experience, I began making sites for family and friends. Most of the sites and javascript apps I created is my art and hobby. Now my purpose is to participate in interesting projects and gain experience to solve of complex front-end tasks.
I am currently available for collaborations. You can hire me as a freelancer or invite me to your team.
